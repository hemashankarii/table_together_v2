About us

We are a team of passionate individuals committed to addressing the issue of food waste in India. Our goal is to create a system that connects food donors with charities and NGOs, while also reducing the environmental impact of food waste.


By donating with us, you are not only helping those in need but also contributing to a sustainable future. Every meal counts, and together we can make a difference. Your generosity will bring hope and nourishment to countless individuals, and we invite you to be part of this meaningful journey.

Ready to make a difference? Donate Now and join the movement against food waste!
